DINNER-STYLE GRILLED CHEESE

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 slices bread
  • 1 slice cheese
  • 1 tabelspoon mayonnaise

DIRECTIONS

  • Heat a small skillet to medium.
  • Using a butter knife, spread half of mayonnaise onto one slice of the bread, place mayo side down in the skillet.
  • Spread remaining mayonnaise onto second slice of bread and place mayonnaise side up on top of cheese
  • Cook 3 minutes, or until bread is toasty brown, turn and repeat.

FRENCH ONION SOUP

INGREDIENTS

  • 5 onions, unpeeled
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • black peeper
  • 1 bay leaf

DIRECTIONS

  • Peel onions and slice 1/8 inch thick,
  • Melt butter in a 6-quart (or larger) stockpot. Add onions; cook, uncovered, over low heat for 1 1/2 hours, stirring occasionally.
  • Stir in pepper, paprika and bay leaf; saute over low heat 10 minutes more, stirring frequently.
  • Reduce heat and simmer slowly for 2 hours.
  • Adjust color to a rich brown with caramel coloring, season with salt. Refrigerate overnight. To serve, heat soup in microwave or on stove top.
  • If desired, pour into ovenproof crocks or bowls.
  • Leftover soup can be frozen.

WALTER'S POTATO, BACON, CORN CHOWDER

INGREDIENTS

  • 8 slices bacon, diced
  • 4 medium potatoes, chopped

DIRECTIONS

BISCUITS

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 cups flour
  • 4 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/3 cup butter
  • 3/4 cup milk

DIRECTIONS

  • Preheat oven to 425 F Mix first 4 ingredients together in a bowl.
  • Cut in butter with a pastry blender or a fork, or two knives, until crumbly.
  • Add milk
  • Roll or pat to 1 inch thick. (I usually pat the dough).
  • Cut into circles with a biscuit cutter or a floured glass.
  • Bake in 425 F for about 10- 12 minutes or until risen and nicely browned. (in my oven it usually takes 15 minutes).
  • This is supposed to make 12, but I almost always only get 11 biscuits, with the size of the glass that I use.

SOFT BREADSTICKS

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 cup warmwater
  • 1/4 cup oil
  • 3 cups bread flour
  • 2 teaspoons yeast

DIRECTIONS

  • Make dough using your favorite method- bread machine, mixer or by hand.
  • Roll out into a 10x12 inch rectangle.
  • Give each strip a twist and place on a greased cookie sheet.
  • Let rise for at least 20 minutes or more if you have time.
  • Bake at 375 for 10-15 minutes.
  • Brush with butter and sprinkle with garlic salt and parmesan cheese.

BROCCOLI CHEESE SOUP

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 cups low sodium chicken broth
  • 3 cups chopped broccoli
  • 1/2 cup chopped celery
  • 2 tabelspoons butter
  • 4 tabelspoons flour
  • 1/2 cup cheese

DIRECTIONS

  • In a medium saucepan, bring broth, broccoli (about 1 med. head will yield 3 cups chopped broccoli) and celery to a boil over high heat.
  • Reduce heat to medium and simmer until broccoli is tender, about 10 minutes.
  • Stir occasionally.
  • Meanwhile, melt butter in a large saucepan over medium heat and stir in flour, salt and pepper; cook for 1 minute.
  • Bring to a boil.
  • Now add broccoli mixture (smooth or chunky) into large saucepan with the milk.
  • Remove from heat and stir in cheese.
  • Serve immediately.

ROSEMARY'S CROCK POT PIZZA

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 cup biscuit mix
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 jar pizza souce
  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese

DIRECTIONS

  • Drizzle olive oil on the bottom of the crock then put about one quarter of your pizza sauce on the bottom of the crock.
  • Mix up your crust and spread on top of the sauce to almost touch the sides then cover with the remaining sauce and coat it all over so it sort of swims in the sauce like a giant soft dumpling.
  • Top with cheese and your chosen meat and veggies, cover and cook on high for about 2 hours. Enjoy!

BAKED POTATO SALAD

INGREDIENTS

  • 4 lbs potatoes
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 medium green pepper
  • 1 medium sweet red pepper
  • 1 bunch green onion
  • 2 large tomatoes
  • 4 hard-boiled eggs
  • 1 cup mayonnnaise
  • 1 tabelspoon vinegar
  • 1 tabelspoon lemon juice

DIRECTIONS

  • In a large bowl, toss the postatoes with oil and dressing mixes.
  • Place into 2 ungreased 13x9x2 inch baking pans.
  • Bake, uncovered at 400 for 45 minutes or until tender.
  • Cool.
  • Transfer to a large bowl, add peppers, onions, tomatoes, eggs and bacon
  • Mix well.
  • Pour over salad and mix gently.
  • Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour.

EASY MANICOTTI

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 cup cheese
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon parsley
  • 1 packet manicotti

DIRECTIONS

  • Combine Ricotta, Mozzarella, grated Parmesan, sugar, egg, nutmeg, parsley, salt and pepper to taste.
  • Stuff uncooked manicotti generously with the cheese mixture, from both ends.
  • Arrange manicotti in a single layer in a baking pan.
  • Bring sauce to a boil and pour over manicotti, covering completely.
  • Cover baking pan with aluminum foil, crimping the edges to seal tightly.
  • Remove foil; sprinkle generously with additional grated cheese.
  • Bake uncovered for 5 minutes more.
